Treatment Services

Our Addiction Treatment Services

Recovery is possible. Our non-judgmental, evidence-based programme treats the whole person — not just the addiction — to build lasting sobriety and a fulfilling life.

🏥
Medical Detox

Medically supervised detoxification to safely manage withdrawal symptoms with 24-hour clinical monitoring and support.

💊
Medication-Assisted Treatment

FDA-approved medications combined with counselling to reduce cravings, prevent relapse, and support long-term recovery.

🧠
Addiction Counselling

Individual and group therapy addressing the root causes of addiction, including trauma, co-occurring mental health disorders, and triggers.

👨‍👩‍👧
Family Programme

Involving loved ones in the recovery process through family therapy, education sessions, and co-dependency counselling.

🌟
Aftercare Planning

A personalised relapse prevention plan and ongoing alumni support to help you maintain sobriety and navigate life's challenges.

🤝
Peer Support Groups

Facilitated peer recovery groups that build community, accountability, and connection with others on the same journey.

Phases of Treatment

Your Recovery Pathway

🌱
Assessment & Detox
Safe, medically supervised withdrawal management and comprehensive addiction assessment.
🔧
Intensive Treatment
Deep therapeutic work addressing root causes, building coping skills, and establishing sobriety foundations.
🏡
Community Reintegration
Gradual return to daily life with structured support, job coaching, and relationship rebuilding.
💪
Ongoing Aftercare
Long-term follow-up, alumni groups, and relapse prevention to sustain recovery indefinitely.
