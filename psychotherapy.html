Therapy Approaches

Methods We Use

Our licensed therapists are trained in multiple evidence-based modalities, allowing us to match the right approach to each individual's unique needs and circumstances.

🔄
Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Identifying and reshaping negative thought patterns and behaviours that contribute to emotional distress and mental health challenges.

🌊
Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)

Building emotional regulation, distress tolerance, mindfulness, and interpersonal effectiveness skills for lasting change.

👁️
EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing to safely process traumatic memories and reduce their emotional impact.

🧘
Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Cultivating present-moment awareness to reduce stress, prevent depressive relapse, and improve overall well-being.

🪑
Psychodynamic Therapy

Exploring how past experiences and unconscious processes shape current behaviours, emotions, and relationships.

🎯
Solution-Focused Therapy

Building on your existing strengths and resources to create practical, goal-oriented solutions and positive change.

FAQs

Common Questions

How often will I need to attend therapy? +
Most clients begin with weekly sessions. As you progress, frequency may reduce to fortnightly or monthly. Your therapist will work with you to determine the right pace for your specific goals.
How do I know which type of therapy is right for me? +
During your first session, your therapist will conduct a thorough assessment and collaboratively determine which approach best suits your needs, preferences, and treatment goals. Sometimes a blend of modalities is most effective.
What if I don't connect with my therapist? +
The therapeutic relationship is crucial to outcomes. If you feel the fit is not right, please let us know. We will support you in finding a better-matched therapist within our team — no questions asked.
