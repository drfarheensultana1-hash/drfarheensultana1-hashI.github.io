Sleep Disorders We Treat

Conditions We Specialise In

Poor sleep affects every aspect of your health — mood, cognition, immunity, and longevity. Our sleep specialists use advanced diagnostics to identify and treat the root cause of your sleep problems.

💤
Sleep Apnoea

Diagnosis and management of obstructive and central sleep apnoea with CPAP therapy, oral appliances, and lifestyle modification.

🌙
Insomnia

CBT-I (Cognitive Behavioural Therapy for Insomnia) — the gold-standard, drug-free treatment for chronic insomnia disorders.

🦵
Restless Leg Syndrome

Assessment and treatment of RLS and periodic limb movement disorder to restore comfortable, uninterrupted sleep.

😵
Narcolepsy

Specialist diagnosis of narcolepsy and hypersomnia conditions with tailored medication and lifestyle management plans.

🕐
Circadian Rhythm Disorders

Shift work disorder, delayed sleep phase syndrome, and jet lag treated with light therapy and chronotherapy.

👻
Parasomnias

Evaluation and treatment of sleepwalking, night terrors, REM sleep behaviour disorder, and sleep paralysis.

Warning Signs

When Should You Seek Help?

😫
Feeling unrefreshed after a full night's sleep most mornings
😴
Excessive daytime sleepiness that affects work or driving
⏰
Taking more than 30 minutes to fall asleep on most nights
😤
Loud snoring or gasping episodes reported by a partner
🧠
Difficulty concentrating or memory problems due to poor sleep
😟
Anxiety about going to bed or fear of not being able to sleep
Diagnostic Process

How We Diagnose Your Sleep Issues

01
Sleep History Consultation
A detailed review of your sleep patterns, medical history, medications, and lifestyle factors that may be affecting your sleep.
02
Sleep Diary & Questionnaires
Validated sleep assessment tools and a 1–2 week sleep diary to objectively capture your sleep patterns and problem areas.
03
Overnight Sleep Study (PSG)
If indicated, a comprehensive overnight polysomnography study to monitor brain waves, oxygen levels, and body movements during sleep.
04
Personalised Treatment Plan
A tailored programme combining behavioural interventions, therapy, and where appropriate, medical treatment to restore healthy sleep.
